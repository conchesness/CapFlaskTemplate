P^5 by Owen and Team

P^5 is a project created by four students.

P^5 is designed to help workers and students manage their work and schedules and battle procrastination.

Our tools include a calculator, grade database, to-do list, study methods, and more.

Students especially struggle with juggling loaded schedules and multiple tasks. We aim to aid students of all ages and studies.

How to use our website

Under posts, you can make a post explaining your thoughts with a field specifically for your feelings. We find that mental health is very important for working.

Grades and grades list can be used to track grades. You can create, edit, and delete grades using this feature. This is to keep student organized and make them log their own progress and face how they are doing.

To use our to do list, you type in you have to do in the bar, then press add. Some examples already exist. You can delete them by hovering over the item and pressing the red X.

Our calculator can be used by typing in the operation and then pressing =, or by using our buttons. It can handle basic problems but won't do your work for you!

Stress relief stores information and resources for study techniques. There is a list of useful time management techniques to try out, as well as YouTube videos on stress-relieving techniques. Enjoy!

The timer helps you to keep track of your time while doing activities.
