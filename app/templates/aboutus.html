About Us

A platform looking to improve teaching quality one evaluation at a time!

Our Impact

Teaching quality has not improved in public schools, yet teaching quality has been shown to have the greatest effect on student achievement. In order to maintain the success of America as a country, we need to ensure the future generation of Americans is well educated. Teaching quality has the greatest effect on student achievement. 10 different studies have shown that a good teacher can improve students learning by up to 0.14 standard deviations. Despite this, federal law is focusing less attention on teacher quality by rolling back on teacher evaluations. This is clearly a mistake as student outcomes have been shown to be most affected by teacher quality.

Our Solution

We believe that having publicly available and regular teacher evaluations will improve teaching quality in US public schools. Our proprietary technology is being used to create an online teacher evaluation platforms that offers a cheap and more effective alternative to the administrative teacher evaluations commonly used in schools. We plan on mkaing these evaluations available to anyone who wants to access it. We hope in the future that we will be able to create algorithms that are able to offer valuable insights for teachers, students, and administrators from the collected data.
