Our Impact

Teaching quality has not improved in public schools, yet teaching quality has been shown to have the greatest effect on student achievement. To maintain the success of America as a country, we need to ensure the future generation of Americans is well educated. 10 different studies have shown that a good teacher can improve students' learning by up to 0.14 standard deviations. Despite this, federal law is focusing less attention on teacher quality by rolling back teacher evaluations. This is flat-out ineffective, we must focus on the crucial teacher-student relationship to better our youth. Through our app, one evaluation at a time, we will make progress to satisfy both sides of the teacher-student relationship.

Our Solution

We believe that having publicly available and regular teacher evaluations will improve teaching quality in US public schools. Our proprietary technology is being used to create an online teacher evaluation platform that offers a cheap and more effective alternative to the administrative teacher evaluations commonly used in schools. We plan on making these evaluations available to whoever wants to access them. We hope that we will be able to create algorithms that can offer valuable insights for teachers, students, and administrators from the collected data shortly.
