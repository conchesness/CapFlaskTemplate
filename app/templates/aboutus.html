About This Course




This course is meant to teach children about the basics of nutrition through articles, videos, quizzes, and interactive activities. It includes three units: Understanding Nutrients, The Journey of Food, and Building Healthy Habits. Each unit will help kids to better understand the what they eat and build a healthy relationship with food. Students will take a nutrition quiz at the beginning of the course to test what they know and then take the same test at the end of the course in order to measure their progress.





Unit 1

In this unit entitled Understanding Nutrients, students will learn what macronutrients and micronutrients are and discover why specific nutrients are important to a personâ€™s diet. Also, they will find out which common foods possess such nutrients.

Unit 2

In Unit 2: The Journey of Food, students will be exposed to the general process the food groups goes through to get from farm to table. By gaining this knowledge, children will feel more connected to the food they eat and better understand the concepts of food processing and farming. Students will even have the opportunity to do an easy gardening experiment of their own!


Unit 3

Lastly, in this unit, Building Healthy Habits, students will learn how to establish healthy eating habits and recognize which foods will be better for them in the long run. They will also be exposed to resources such as government food assistance programs, food banks, and tips for shopping for affordable healthy groceries that make healthy eating more accessible.





NOTE TO PARENTS:

The internet is a huge and often dangerous place for children. Please make sure your child stays safe and on task while completing this course by checking in on them every once in a while, offering help during optional activities, and making sure they have sufficient water and food to allow their brains to function properly.
